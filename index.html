About Neigle

I work at the University of York as the Computing Officer for the Archaeology Department. I look after the IT for the department, build PCs, and work on archaeological projects like Star Carr and Sicily in Transition.

I also build websites for projects and businesses. I love front-end web development, and I'm trying to get good at it. Have a look in the Projects section to check out some of my work. You'll also find some examples of artefact photography that I've taken.

I'm involved in 3D modelling of objects too. I use photogrammetry to make visualisations of archaeological objects. Check them out at Sketchfab. I wrote a manual on the process of creating a model using Agisoft Photoscan.
